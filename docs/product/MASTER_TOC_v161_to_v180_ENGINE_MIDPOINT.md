# MASTER TOC v161-v180 — Engine Midpoint Road

## Meaning

This road builds the operational engine layer after the v160 architecture lock and v160.1 build audit.

## Srpski

Ovaj put gradi operativni sloj engine-a nakon v160 architecture lock-a i v160.1 build audit-a.
