# Homepage Product Proof Master — v131.0.0

## Purpose

The homepage must stop behaving like a thin idea page and start behaving like the front door of a practical finance product.

## Product definition

AndyAI Finance Engine is a practical, reviewable, evidence-backed finance workflow system.

It helps explain how messy financial inputs can become model previews, assumptions notes, approval checkpoints, and evidence-backed output.

## Why this matters

Financial users do not trust vague AI promises. They need to see:
- what goes in
- what comes out
- what remains blocked
- who reviews the output
- what evidence is preserved
- what the next action is

## Public homepage improvements

v131.0.0 adds:
- stronger hero
- Example in Action workflow
- proof cards linking to deeper routes
- clear input/output explanation
- strong "No blind financial autopilot" trust block
- audience clarity for founders, finance leads, and analysts
- final pilot CTA

## Acceptance criteria

- homepage explains the product in ordinary language
- proof routes are visible from the homepage
- CTA routes are visible from the homepage
- finance trust boundary is clear
- build passes
- tag v131.0.0 is pushed
