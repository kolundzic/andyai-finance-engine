# v16.6.0 — Client Objection Handling Pack

## “Is this just another chatbot?”

No. The point is not chat. The point is governed financial workflow with proof, review, and approval.

## “Can it make financial decisions automatically?”

No. The public product layer is read-only and approval-based.

## “Where is the safety?”

The safety is in the boundary: no writes, no billing, no checkout, no webhooks, no provider calls in this layer.

## “What is the first pilot?”

A controlled workflow using sample or approved business inputs, evidence review, and human approval.
