# Public Business System Explained

The public business system is the visible product surface of AndyAI Finance Engine.

It shows a visitor:

1. what the product is;
2. what proof exists;
3. how a pilot could work;
4. what safety boundaries exist;
5. why human approval remains central;
6. what future runtime gates may look like.

It is intentionally static for now.

That means the product can be understood before it becomes operationally dangerous.
