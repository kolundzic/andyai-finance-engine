# AndyAI Finance Engine v4.5.0 — Membership Access Policy

## Purpose

This checkpoint defines the first membership and access policy for AndyAI Finance Engine.

It does not activate subscriptions, checkout, payment providers, or billing enforcement.

## Membership States

| State | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Free Preview | Public demo and explanation access |
| Professional | Individual professional workspace access |
| Business Team | Team workspace and approval workflow access |
| Enterprise Custom | Custom governance and integration path |
| Manual Review | Operator review required before access continues |
| Suspended | Access paused because review, billing, or safety action is required |

## Access Principle

Membership controls product access.

Membership does not control truth, audit, safety, or human responsibility.

## Free Preview Access

Free Preview may include:

- Public product explanation
- Demo workflow
- Sample financial modeling outputs
- Trust and proof pages
- Documentation preview

Free Preview should not include:

- Private project persistence
- Team workspace
- Paid export history
- Live billing features

## Professional Access

Professional access may include:

- Private project creation
- Model assumption capture
- Draft financial model generation
- Export-ready summaries
- Basic audit visibility

Professional access must still require human review for serious outputs.

## Business Team Access

Business Team access may include:

- Shared workspace
- Team member roles
- Approval workflow
- Usage limits
- Audit trail visibility
- Export history

Business Team access must separate operator, reviewer, and owner responsibilities.

## Enterprise Custom Access

Enterprise Custom access may include:

- Custom onboarding
- Advanced governance review
- Private policy planning
- Integration discussion
- Dedicated reporting

Enterprise Custom is not a simple checkout tier.

## Manual Review State

Manual Review is used when a user, workspace, payment event, export request, or model output needs operator inspection.

This state protects the system from treating automation as final authority.

## Suspended State

Suspended access blocks monetized functionality until the issue is resolved.

Suspension may be caused by billing failure, abuse risk, policy concern, or governance review.

## Human Approval Rule

Paid access must not bypass human approval.

The system may generate financial model drafts, but a human must approve whether the model is suitable for use.

## Canonical Formula

Access can be sold.

Trust cannot be sold.

Trust must be designed, verified, and protected.
