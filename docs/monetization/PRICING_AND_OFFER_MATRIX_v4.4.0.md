# AndyAI Finance Engine v4.4.0 — Pricing and Offer Matrix

## Purpose

This checkpoint defines the first pricing and offer matrix for AndyAI Finance Engine.

It does not activate payments.

## Offer Layers

| Layer | Audience | Promise |
|---|---|---|
| Free Preview | Visitors and early users | Demo, explanation, and trust-building |
| Professional | Founders, consultants, small agencies | Structured financial model drafts |
| Business Team | Teams and client operators | Shared workflow, approvals, and audit |
| Enterprise Custom | Larger organizations | Custom governance and integration path |

## Gate Rule

No pricing number becomes canonical until usage limits, export value, billing provider, cancellation language, and human approval rules are defined.

## Canonical Principle

The customer does not pay for magic.

The customer pays for a governed financial modeling workflow that helps them think, verify, explain, and decide.
