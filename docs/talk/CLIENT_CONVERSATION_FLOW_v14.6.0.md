# v14.6.0 — Client Conversation Flow

## Opening

“AndyAI Finance Engine is a governed financial modeling system. It helps prepare structured financial outputs, but every important action remains reviewable and approval-based.”

## Client questions

### What does it do?

It organizes business inputs, model runs, assumptions, evidence, and approval steps.

### Why is it safer than a normal chatbot?

Because it separates proof from execution.

### Can it write to the database now?

Public routes cannot write data.

### Can it make payments or billing actions?

No. Billing, checkout, webhooks, and paid runtime are blocked.

### What is the business value?

Faster model preparation, clearer review, and stronger trust in the numbers.
