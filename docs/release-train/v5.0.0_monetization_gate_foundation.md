# v5.0.0 — Monetization Gate Foundation

## Summary

v5.0.0 completes the first controlled monetization foundation for AndyAI Finance Engine.

This is not a live payment launch.

It is the foundation that allows the product to move toward monetization without breaking trust, auditability, or human approval.

## Included Foundation

- v4.2.0 Recovery Checkpoint
- v4.3.0 Monetization Gate Docs
- v4.4.0 Pricing and Offer Matrix
- v4.5.0 Membership Access Policy
- v4.6.0 Usage Quota Policy
- v4.7.0 Billing Adapter Contract
- v4.8.0 Safety and Export Rules
- v4.9.0 v5 Readiness Checklist

## Safety

No checkout, billing provider, webhook, payment SDK, subscription enforcement, payment secret, or live monetization behavior is introduced.

## Trust Position

The monetization layer must remain subordinate to the trust layer.

Paid access may unlock workflow capacity.

Paid access must not bypass human review, assumption visibility, audit trail, export warnings, or operator accountability.

## Final Canonical Formula

Financial modeling can be automated.

Financial responsibility cannot be outsourced.
